14 December 2016 - UPDATE

Dear Friends of the Monastery,

we wish to THANK YOU all, for the many initiatives organized in our aid, for the various offerings, small and big, that you have donated and for all that you bear in your hearts for us in the future. Thanks to you, we have felt surrounded as always by affection and esteem, because of the service of prayer that we offer for you. May the Lord bless you!
We ask you to remain in contact with us through the website of the Monastery www.monasterodiacquapendente.it where periodically you may follow the fundraising being carried out and from where we will also update you about our situation. In the meanwhile, we are still in an initial phase, where by means of the necessary inspections and plans, a preliminary project shall be made in order to understand the magnitude of the expense. The B.V.M. Immaculate will help us and Saint Francis, who never fails to show his signs of extraordinary protection of our Monastery, shall intervene in our support!

To all we wish a Holy Christmas, while we shall remember you all and your dear families at the feet of Baby Jesus.

The Mother Abbess and the monastic community of Acquapendente
October 2016 - Urgent Communiqué

On 30-31 May 2016, in the aftermath of an earthquake that struck the zone called “Tuscia”, and after repeated seismic shocks that continued in Latium in the months that followed, our Church, the apse, the Monastic Choir (enclosure) and a part of our Dormitory have been seriously damaged. In order to provide for the securing of these premises, the redoing of the roofing and consolidation of the vault, we are raising up funds. The amount we need to start the works on our part is impossible for us to reach with our own meager means, but we rely on the generosity of all, those who know us and many others who, notwithstanding their not being familiar with our monastery, would wish to unite themselves to us.

For those who wish to help us, the Community shall pray every day, presenting to the Lord the prayer intentions of our benefactors even with the offering of Holy Mass once a month. It is also possible, by means of an email, that you specify particular prayer intentions that you may wish to recommend to our monastic Community.

May God bless you and may the B.V.M. Immaculate reward you for your kindness!

The Mother Abbess and the monastic community of Acquapendente
