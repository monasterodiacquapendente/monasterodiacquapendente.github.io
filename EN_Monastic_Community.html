Our family of Poor Clares of the Immaculate is a new branch of the Order of St. Clare of Assisi (Poor Clares), wholly contemplative. We profess the observance of the Holy Gospel according to the Rule of St. Clare confirmed by Innocent IV, along with our own particular Constitutions which call for the vow of consecration to the Immaculate.
We do not belong to any Institute or religious Family, but exclusively to the Order of St. Clare of Assisi.
We are united spiritually to the First Order of St. Francis and to all the Monasteries of our Order; furthermore, we share fraternal communion with the Secular Franciscan Order, and in a special way with the Secular Franciscans of Assisi, united to our monastery with a spiritual “twinning”.
